About the mountain lake

Our mountain lake is a pearl hidden among majestic mountain ranges. The crystal clear water reflecting the snow-capped peaks creates a unique atmosphere of peace and harmony with nature.

The lake was formed thousands of years ago as a result of melting glaciers and is still fed by the purest springs from the high mountain slopes. The water temperature rarely rises above 15°C even in summer, which preserves its unique purity.

How to visit?

You can get to the lake on foot from the nearest campsite. The route is of medium difficulty, about 8 km long. We recommend that you take it with you:

  • Comfortable trekking shoes
  • Water and food supply
  • Warm clothes (the weather in the mountains is changeable)
  • A camera for unforgettable pictures
